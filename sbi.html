The Journey

It started as a question no one on campus was answering: who actually builds sustainably, and at scale? I founded the Sustainable Building Initiative to be that answer — and grew it into a UT Swing Out Award finalist organization.

"The hardest part wasn't the engineering. It was getting 90+ people from different disciplines to move as one — and holding the line on 100% of our sustainability mandates."

The Struggle & The Result

Directing four large-scale projects at once — a ranch complex, two sustainable residences, and a 23-floor high-rise — meant constant tradeoffs between cost, schedule, and standards. We delivered $800K in sustainable construction without compromising the mission, and established an international presence by managing a $25M project pipeline.
