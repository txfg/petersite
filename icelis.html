The Journey

I programmed the core compliance modules of the firm's ESG tool from Global Reporting Initiative (GRI) research — and built Icelis Clocked In, an internal time-and-activity tracking system that recorded exactly when employees were working and on which assignment.

"Once we could see the real hours, the conversation changed — every cost decision was backed by data instead of assumption."

The Struggle & The Result

Before the system, billable hours were submitted on trust and ran inflated. The systems I built to capture and analyze real activity gave leadership evidence-backed visibility into where time was actually spent — cutting logged hours 23% on day one and settling near a 28% reduction as only genuinely billable time was counted. Alongside it, I restructured the Environmental Master Folder to ISO-certification readiness.
